What is Brainstorming?


"Brainstorming is a group creativity technique by which efforts are made to find a conclusion for a specific problem by gathering a list of ideas spontaneously contributed by its members." And we also implment a similar this website is built for people who are creative or are working in groups to find ideas and simply just to hang out with others, Brainstorming is connected throughout the world so that means that you can chat with anyone in the world when they are online.
